## Your 5-Minute Journey: Step by Step

### Minute 1: Describe Your Vision

Head to [youmake.dev](https://youmake.dev) and describe what you want to build. Be as specific or as vague as you like:

> "A portfolio website for a photographer with a dark theme, image gallery, contact form, and about page"

Or keep it simple:

> "A clean landing page for my consulting business"

The AI understands context, intent, and even aesthetic preferences. This is [vibe coding](/blog/vibe-coding-future-2026) in action—you describe the *vibe*, and AI handles the code.

### Minute 2: Watch AI Build

Youmake's AI agent gets to work immediately:

- **Generates the frontend** with modern React components
- **Creates responsive layouts** that work on all devices
- **Implements your requested features** (forms, galleries, animations)
- **Applies professional design patterns** automatically

You can watch the progress in real-time through the isolated development environment.

### Minute 3: Preview and Refine

Your app appears in a live preview. Not quite right? Just say so:

> "Make the header sticky and add a gradient to the hero section"

The AI iterates instantly. No need to understand CSS—just describe what you want changed. This conversational approach is [why vibe coding is replacing no-code](/blog/no-code-dead-vibe-coding) tools.

### Minute 4: Final Touches

Add finishing touches through natural language:

- "Add SEO meta tags for 'photography portfolio Los Angeles'"
- "Make the contact form send to my email"
- "Add social media links in the footer"

Every request is processed in seconds.

### Minute 5: Deploy to Production

One click. That's it.

Youmake handles:
- **SSL certificates** (automatic HTTPS)
- **CDN distribution** (fast loading worldwide)
- **Domain connection** (use your own domain or a free subdomain)
- **Security hardening** (production-ready configuration)

Your app is live. Share the URL. You're done.
